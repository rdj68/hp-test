Benefits of Yoga for Children:-

Yoga helps children manage their anxiety.

Yoga improves children's emotional regulation.

Yoga boosts children's self-esteem.

Yoga increases children's body awareness and mindfulness.

Yoga enhances children's concentration and memory.

Yoga develops children's strength and flexibility.

Suryanamaskar:-

Surya Namaskar, or "Sun Salutation," is a series of yoga poses that are typically performed in sequence.

It is believed that this practice can have a number of positive effects on the brain, including reducing stress and anxiety, increasing focus and concentration, and promoting feelings of calm and relaxation.

Some research has also suggested that Surya Namaskar may be beneficial in reducing symptoms of depression and improving cognitive function.
